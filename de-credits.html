Lookup Transfer Equivalencies

Dual Enrollment transfer credits map to specific Georgia Tech course codes. Because this process is highly dependent on your college, please use the following official tools to verify your transfer credits:

  • GT Transfer Equivalency Catalog (OSCAR) — Lookup the exact course code matching your transfer institution.
  • GT Transfer Credit Admission Policy — Review policies on grades, credit limits, and accreditation requirements.
